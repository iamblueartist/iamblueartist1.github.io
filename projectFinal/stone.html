I’ve developed a rather
Odd compulsion lately.
It seems I’ve been swallowing stones
All shapes and sizes,
I lift them up, with forced hesitation
And I close my lips around
The cold, hard exterior.
Take a skipping stone
Make a wish
Put it to rest in my stomach.
Calcified thoughts.
This eternal lump lodged in my throat,
Of words I never could say.
I’ve been moving rather sluggish recently.
Some may say its my diet,
But my indigestion only weighs me down
So I don’t float away
